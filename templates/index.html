About Lora Life

At LORA LIFE, we are transforming the livestock industry with cutting-edge technology that ensures healthier animals, higher productivity, and smarter farm management. Our platform provides real-time health monitoring, AI-driven diagnostics, and seamless veterinary access, helping farmers detect diseases early, reduce losses, and optimize animal care.

Our Services

Real-Time Monitoring

Our system provides real-time tracking of your livestock’s vital signs, including temperature, heart rate, and movement. With AI-powered analytics, farmers receive instant alerts for any unusual activity, helping detect diseases before they spread.

AI-Powered Analytics

Using AI, we analyze livestock behavior to detect potential health issues early. This helps reduce losses and improve farm productivity.

Veterinary Booking

Farmers can book veterinary services directly through our platform, ensuring timely medical intervention for their livestock.

Health Insights

Access detailed reports on livestock health trends, allowing for better decision-making and improved herd management.

Automated Alerts

Get instant alerts via SMS or app notifications when anomalies are detected in your livestock’s health.

Cloud-Based Data

Store and retrieve livestock health records anytime, anywhere, for better tracking and farm management.
